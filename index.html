💧 Waterfall Explorer — Help

Choosing a mode

  • Parking access — finds waterfalls that have a parking area within your chosen distance. Blue markers have parking; dark grey named waterfalls and light grey unnamed ones do not.
  • Trail access — finds waterfalls reachable from a hiking path within your chosen distance. Green markers are within range; others are hidden.

Setting a location

  • Type any place name in the Search location box and press Enter or click Locate.
  • Or click anywhere on the map to move the search center to that point.
  • Or tap the crosshair button in the top-left of the map to center on your current GPS location (requires location permission).

Sliders

  • Search radius — how far from the center to look for waterfalls. The dashed circle on the map updates live.
  • Max parking / trail dist — the maximum distance a waterfall can be from a parking area or trail to be considered accessible.

Waterfall popups

  • Hover over any marker to see the waterfall name, access distance, and a link to its OpenStreetMap page.
  • Move the mouse into the popup to keep it open and click the OSM link.

Data source

  • All data is fetched live from OpenStreetMap via the Overpass API. Coverage varies by region.
